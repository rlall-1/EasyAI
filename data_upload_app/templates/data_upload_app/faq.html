Welcome to The Data Insights Platform FAQ


Q. What is this platform?
Ans. The platform is for the business user who wants to leverage ML but does not have the time to code.

Q. How do I use it?
Ans. For example, you want to predict the price of a house. Take the data that you have and put it in an excel file. Put all the factors that you think are responsible for the price of the house like size, age etc. The last column should contain the price of the house. Save this as a .csv file. Now, upload your file for generating the model.
